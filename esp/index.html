About

ESP contracting is a contracting company that will provide you with beautifully constructed renovations and/or additions to your home (house or condominium). ESP contracting is an experienced renovation company that shows dedication to every aspect of the renovation process.  We understand that renovating your home can be stressful, and we strive to ensure that all work done is with the greatest care.  We have been serving the greater Toronto community and beyond for over 10 years.  ESP Contracting offers you a wide variety of skilled professionals including carpenters, plumbers, electricians, cabinet makers, drywall/tapers and painters, who will help bring any project, whether big or small, to fruition.

From replacing doors or windows, to complete home renovations, we can help turn your dream into reality.  We know that even the smallest renovation can add value to your home, and we take this task very seriously.  We stand by our quality work and will guide you through every step of the renovation process.  We have a proven track record of customer satisfaction.   We build strong relationships with our clients, and we pride ourselves on developing long lasting, trustworthy relationships. As our motto states: Building your home is our passion.
